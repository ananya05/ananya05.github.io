Sanfrancisco

  • Crooked Street

    • This attraction is located on Lombard street, San Francisco. It is very exciting as you drive down this street which has it peculiar crooked shape. Many houses and flower gardens are on the side of the street.Biking and walking is also allowed.

  • Ghiradelli

    • This chocolate factory is located on Ghiradelli square, California. It is great to end a active day with a nice, hot chocolate fudge sundae. You get every variety of sweets from ice crem sundaes, to milkshakes.

  • Golden Gate Bridge

    • THis bridge is located on ,California. It is called the golden gate bridge because of California Gold Rush. It is a suspension bridge located on California's Bay. You can drive across the great bridge in your car, watching the beautiful bay.
